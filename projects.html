American Greetings eCards - iOS

(October 2011 - March 2012)

Description:


Send digital greetings from the comfort of your iPhone, iPad or iPod Touch. You get instant access to greetings featuring animation, music, and the ability to add your own photos and words. Any card can be delivered via email or posted to Facebook, and there are no per-card charges – EVER! We regularly release new cards without requiring an app update, so you’ll always have the cards you need for holidays, for celebrations, and even for the everyday reasons you stay in touch with friends, family and loved ones.
